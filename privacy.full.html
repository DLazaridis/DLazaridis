Intro

This policy gives a description on how Dimitrios Lazaridis (“me”, “I”, or “my”) collects, stores, and uses information about you applying to all of my games on mobile devices, PCs or on other platforms in order to provide you and develop my products.

All my activities regarding marketing and advertising and other services (“Services”) are governed by this Privacy Policy on all platforms that using or accessing a Service means you have agreed to the use of your information in compliance with this Privacy Policy.

If there’s any concern regarding your providing information to me or you disagree it being used as described in this Privacy Policy, please do not install or use the Services.

This Privacy Policy was last updated on 03.10.2018.

Information Collection

Your information is collected at the start of using my games and/or other Services, such as dlazaridis.github.io and my website. Personal data include, but are not limited to: your name, country and region, address, email address, device information, and game play information. I use this data to provide you with better experience of my games and Services in purposes of optimization, preventing fraud, and for marketing and advertising. I may also collect information from advertising platforms and partners and other third parties such as information about purchases and interests.

In more detail, the collecting information consists how you play my games (such as which item and how much of them got used, levels attempted and purchases made) and any permitted information you grant when creating an account with me and connecting to your social network accounts.

I may collect and process the following types of information:

  1. The playtime of yours using my app.
  2. If you have left the game on a specific level or if you have cleared it.
  3. The score you earned in levels.
  4. The items you use, purchase, and/or own.
  5. Information I collect via cookies and other similar technologies, as explained further below;
  6. Your interactions with me on my social media channels;
  7. Information as further set out in this Privacy Policy, including Social sharing features , Marketing and Advertising;
  8. In any contact between you and me, I may keep a record in purposes to give best answers to provide you a better experience using my app.
  9. From my other third party companies who have your consent or have other legal right to share granted information with me (advertising networks and publishing partners, platforms). This may include your interests, other games you played, demographic and general location information. I will use this information as described in this Privacy Policy.
  10. Purchase history on behalf of my payment service providers to keep a record on if a purchase has been successfully made. I do not collect credit card information.
  11. The type of your device, IP address, your game ID when using the app, and the country or region that you’re playing in.
  12. If you choose not to connect to your social network accounts when using my games, I collect anonymous information and still there will be device level identifiers and other information described here.

I may ask for your approval to collect other information in some circumstances from you or your device. Or any other similar activities be done I will inform you what information I am to collect, the reason I collect it, and how I will be using that information.

For the other purposes I may share your information with third party publishers who develop and provide games and other Services to your on our behalf.

I also use the information collected for analysis reason on an anonymized or aggregated basis to develop the products, to analyze the performance of my games, to troubleshoot and to enhance the quality and optimize the games to guarantee the best experience to those who use my Services as a whole.

Optimizing My Services to Provide
I analyze and optimize to provide you the best of my products using your information included with use and analysis of aggregated data to verify that my websites work properly on all devices.

Customer Services
Your information data delivered or provided directly to us in communications in terms of customer service and through its channels will be used. Analytics and Research
I collect information using analytics tools such as third party analytics tools to see how you play my games or use my Services. Those tools may contain cookies or other similar tracking technologies. I may use your data for research and analytics purposes to engage with you directly. Social and Community
There are social and community channels I use to communicate with my players, I will receive your information only when you choose to interact with those channels, information be used in connection with those channels. We will more closely engage with you through those channels and may republish your posts to those channels.

Marketing

I may serve ads based on your information I collect about you within my Services or place ads on third party websites, apps and internet-connected devices. Information I may have about you such as: ad identifiers and other non-personal information collected from your device, how you interact with my game and Services, the game play information, your age, country or region, gender, and other information collected from third parties with legal right to share it with me are used to make sure that you only see marketing from me that might be of interest to you.

Marketing Partners
There are various third party marketing partners I use when I market my games in media. With the help of your information collected when you use their services, they make assumptions to ensure that most relevant advertising that you would prefer to see is presented. I am not responsible for data use of my marketing partners though there is always a legal basis required for them to use your data for marketing purposes and to provide you the details on how it is being used.

Advertising

My games are included with third party advertising for their products and services. I and my partners use your information to improve advertising and targeting to measure the relevant third party ads to show in my games. Information I may have about you such as: ad identifiers and other non-personal information collected from your device, how you interact with my game and Services, the game play information, your age, country or region, gender, and other information collected from third parties with legal right to share it with me are used to make sure that you only see ads from me that might be of interest to you.

Advertising Partners
Non-personal identifiers and other information collected from your device when you play my games or use my Services are shared with advertising partners in order to technically deliver the ads to your device. Such information may be combined with collected information of my advertising partners own to serve you with more relevant ads and to enhance the accuracy of their targeting.

Ad Identifiers
I use ad identifiers that are non-permanent, non-personal identifiers such as the Android and/or Apple’s ID for advertising associated with your device, and similar technologies such as tracking pixels within ads to provide interest-based advertising. These technologies enable me and my third party advertising partners to recognize you and your device, allow our Services to interact with a third party social network or platform and help my payment processors to work it out your payment instructions.

How to reset your preferences for advertising
You may reset your ad identifier being used for interest-based advertising by changing the settings of your device usually found under “privacy” or “ads”. You can also opt-out from your information being collected if you live in the USA, Europe or Canada by visiting http://www.aboutads.info/choices/ (for US users), http://www.youronlinechoices.com/ (for European users) or http://youradchoices.ca/choices (for Canadian users). The full use of my Services may not be available if you choose to adjust your preferences.

Your Rights and Controls

Right of Access
If you wish to know which information I collect and hold about you please contact me so I can verify your identity to provide you with the requested information. If the information hold personal information about someone else I may not allow to give all the information asked for.

Information Correctness
If there is any information I hold about you that you would like to correct, please let me know at maintenance.dlazaridis@gmail.com I will make corrections as soon as possible.

If you wish to delete any information about you, please contact me knowing that in order to complete the process you must delete my games from your mobile devices. There may be some information I will be still holding for other purposes such as protecting legal rights or maintaining financial records and marketing suppression lists. Any anonymised form that holds of your information may be retained as well.

You can deactivate your account you created in my game by contacting me. Please note that I may ask you about other information such as your mobile device information and once it is completely deactivated your unspent virtual items may not be remained.

Restriction of Processing
You may request a restriction of the processing of your data in some circumstances.

Right to Object

Direct Marketing Opt Out
